# Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why can't I send a join request?
A: You must be logged in.

Q: Why don't I see the "Post Project" button?
A: Only logged-in users can post projects.

Q: Why can't I message a user?
A: Messaging requires login.

Q: Why is "Close Project" button missing?
A: Only project creators can close their projects.

Q: Why are there no recommended projects for me?
A: Check that your profile includes skills and a tech stack.

Q: Can I edit a project after creating it?
A: No. You must delete and recreate it.

Q: Can I join my own project?
A: No, the system does not allow sending join requests to your own project.
